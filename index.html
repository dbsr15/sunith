About Me

Dhulipalla Narendra Kumar

Dhulipalla Narendra Kumar was born to Smt. Prameela Devi & Shri Dhulipalla Veeraiah Chowdary, in the year 1967 in Guntur District. Dhulipalla Narendra Kumar completed his SSC from Ananthavarapadu village and continued his Intermediate Education from Loyola College Vijayawada. He pursued his B.Tech from the University of Mysore in the year 1989.


Narendra was drawn to politics at the early age of 26 due to the sudden demise of his father Late Sri Dhulipalla Veeraiah Chowdary Garu & started his career in Telugu Desam Party (TDP) and elected Member of Legislative Assembly (APLA) from Ponnuru Constituency in the year 1994 & he WON for the 5 Consecutive terms till 2019 continued the legacy of his father. He was elevated to the position of opposition party Whip of APLA in the year 2009. He worked as the President of Telugu Raithu an affiliated body of TDP. He also worked as the Guntur District TDP President from 2002 â€“ 2004. He is one of the senior-most leaders in the Telugu Desam Party and also Co-ordinator for Eluru and Vijayawada parliament segments.


Dhulipalla Narendra was elected as the Chairman of Sangam Milk Producers Company Limited in the year 2010. Narendra had come out with many reforms in sectors of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry. He started empowering the farmers and as Chairman of Sangam Dairy he had come up with many beneficiary schemes and he extended his full support to farmers. He had started DVC Hospital & Research Centre on behalf of Sri Dhulipalla Veeraiah Chowdary Memorial Trust at Vadlamudi, Guntur District for the welfare of Dairy Farmers.
